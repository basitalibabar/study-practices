My Favourite Activity

I like to learn about Astronomy. It is a very interesting subject in which you learn about existence of new galaxies and planets. It also shows how large different galaxies exist compared to our milky way. The most interesting objects in the universe are pulsars and blackholes which come to existence when a star dies.
